Welcome to day one of my blog. While I may occsionally muse about things unrelated to coding, my primary goal is to document my work on my web page and other coding projects.

My long-term goal is to make this a "proper" blog, and pulling entries from a database. However, in the short term I will be making manual entries directly in the html.

Today's I focused primarily on creating this blog page, including links to the home page and visual styling. I am quite happy with how it looks. I was expecially happy that I was able to find a picture of "The Walrus and the Carpenter" to accompany the title of my blog.

My secondary objective today was to fix an issue with my Barbequugle app, as it was displaying multiple results of the same restaurant when multple matches were found in a search. This was easily remedied by changing the JPQL query in the repository to include 'DISTINCT'.
